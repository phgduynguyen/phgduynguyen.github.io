Biography

I am Phuong-Duy Nguyen (Jeffrey). I received my B.A. (major in Natural Resources Economics) from Can Tho University, Vietnam in 2018. I then worked as a research assistant at Department of Environmental & Resource Economics, School of Economics, Can Tho University for more than three years.

During this period, I have gained a special attraction to agricultural, environmental economics, and their applications on assessing development policies in the changing Mekong Delta of Vietnam.

From May 2021, I started working at VCCI - Can Tho Branch as a project associate.
